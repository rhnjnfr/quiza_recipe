Ube Crinkle Cookie

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 c. (210 g.) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1 large egg, room temperature
  • 1 c. (1 sticks) unsalted butter, melted, and cooled
  • 1/4 c. (55 g.) light brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 c. (150 g.) granulated sugar
  • 1/2 c (150g) Ube halaya
  • 1 tsp. pure vanilla extract
  • 3 tsp. ube extract, such as McCormick
  • 1 c. (115 g.) powdered sugar

Directions

    Step 1

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°. Line 2 rimmed baking sheets with parchment. In a medium bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, and salt.

    2. Step 2

  2. In a large bowl, whisk egg, butter, brown sugar, and 1/2 cup granulated sugar. Add ube halaya and ube extract and whisk until fully incorporated.

    3. Step 3

  3. Add dry ingredients to ube mixture. Using a flexible spatula, mix until flour is incorporated and dough is shiny, about 30 seconds.

    4. Step 4

  4. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 1 hour or up to 1 day.

    5. Step 5

  5. In 2 separate shallow bowls, place powdered sugar and remaining 1 cup granulated sugar. Scoop 20 (2 tablespoons; 35 grams) portions dough and roll into balls. Working with a few at a time, roll in granulated sugar, then in powdered sugar. Arrange 10 balls on prepared sheets, spacing 2" apart.

    6. Step 6

  6. Bake cookies until edges begin to set but center is still slightly soft, 9 to 11 minutes. Let cool 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack and let cool completely. Repeat with remaining dough.
